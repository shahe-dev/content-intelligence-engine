# **App Name**: Content Intelligence Engine

## Core Features:

- Market Intelligence Aggregator: Scrapes and aggregates real estate news, regulatory updates, and forum discussions from specified sources (e.g., ArabianBusiness, DLD, Reddit) to identify trending topics and demand signals. Acts as a tool by suggesting insights.
- SERP and Keyword Analysis: Analyzes search engine results pages (SERPs) and keyword performance using Ahrefs and SEMrush APIs to identify keyword opportunities, track competitor rankings, and uncover search gaps.
- LLM Visibility Tracker: Queries LLMs (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude) with key real estate queries and extracts entities, answers, and citation styles. Tracks whether your content appears in AI overviews and identifies opportunities for LLM optimization.
- Competitor Content Monitoring: Monitors competitor websites for new content, backlinks, and campaigns using Ahrefs, VisualPing, and Screaming Frog. Acts as a tool by providing competitive intelligence.
- Content Scoring and Prioritization: Scores potential content topics based on trend velocity, search demand, LLM presence, competitive gap, and revenue alignment. Generates a prioritized editorial roadmap with titles, keywords, search intent, and CTA recommendations.
- AI-Assisted Content Blueprinting: Uses GPT and SERP data to generate skeleton drafts and outlines for content pieces. Includes target keywords, semantic clusters, on-page elements, and schema markup.Acts as a tool by using generated briefs for writers.
- Performance and Authority Tracking: Tracks content performance metrics (rankings, LLM inclusion, backlinks, behavior signals) and identifies content gaps. Generates a 'Content Authority Scorecard' and 'LLM Visibility Report' to inform content refinement and optimization efforts.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3F51B5) to convey authority and trust in the real estate market.
- Background color: Light grey (#F0F2F5) for a clean, professional look that emphasizes content readability in both light and dark mode.
- Accent color: Teal (#009688) for interactive elements and call-to-action buttons, providing a modern and distinct touch.
- Body text: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif font for a balance of modernity and readability. Headline Text: 'Playfair' for elegance.
- Use professional and consistent icons to represent different content categories and data points.
- Prioritize clear and structured layouts for presenting complex real estate data and market insights.
- Subtle transitions and loading animations to enhance user experience without being distracting.
